Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Pulse is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit, upload, or sync your data to any external servers or cloud services.

Data Collection

We do not collect, track, or have access to any of your personal information or habit data. Your data stays on your device and is never shared with us or any third parties.

Pulse does not require an account and does not show ads.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Your usage of the app is completely private.

App Store Privacy Label

The App Store privacy label for Pulse states "Data Not Collected." Pulse does not collect or link data to you. All habit data remains on your device.

Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can export your habits as CSV files for backup purposes. Deleting the app removes your habit data. A small amount of subscription-related data (such as trial start date) is stored in the iOS Keychain and may persist after deletion to prevent misuse.

iOS Widget

The iOS Home Screen widget accesses your habit data stored locally on your device using iOS App Groups. This data sharing happens entirely on your device and does not involve any external communication.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this document and the app version.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store.
