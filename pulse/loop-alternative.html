Common questions

What's the best habit tracker for iPhone?

Pulse is built specifically for iPhone with a focus on simplicity and privacy. No account required, no cloud sync, no ads — just habit tracking with Home Screen widgets, streaks, and flexible goals.

I'm switching from Android to iPhone. What should I use?

Pulse offers the same core experience you'd expect from a great Android habit tracker: simplicity, privacy, and focused tracking. You'll find flexible habit frequencies, Home Screen widgets, streaks, and CSV export.

Can I import my habit data into Pulse?

Pulse supports CSV import, so you can transfer your history from other habit trackers. Some manual formatting of columns may be needed depending on the source app.

Is Pulse free?

Pulse offers a free trial so you can try everything before deciding. After the trial, a subscription unlocks all features. No tiers, no upsells, no ads.

Why isn't Pulse open source?

Pulse is independently developed. The subscription model supports ongoing development and keeps the app ad-free with no data collection. You still own all your data and can export it anytime.
